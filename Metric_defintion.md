Explanation of the Data for Analysis
Customer:
● CustomerID: unique identifier per customer
● Product_Name: name of the product signed up for
● Signup_Date: the date a customer signed up for the product
● Channel: the marketing channel the customer came through to sign up for
the product
● First_Activation_Date: the date the customer first activated in the given
product
● First_Purchase_Date: first purchase in the given product
● Cancel_Date: the date a customer cancels their subscription. May not be
applicable to products that are not subscription offerings
Usage:
● CustomerID: unique identifier per customer
● Product_name: name of the product used
● Event_Date: date of the action
● Action_type_id: type of the action or feature used in the product. Please
replace these action types to the key actions of the product.
● Usage_count: number of times the customer did that action on a given date
Things to Keep in Mind
● Clearly state any business assumptions you are making of the data.
● Your audience is a mix of technical and non-technical people (marketers,
PMs). Prioritize business insights over mundane technical details.
